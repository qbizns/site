About Natural Harvest

Learn more about our products, hydroponic farming methods, and how we ensure the highest quality vegetables.
  • What is hydroponic farming?
    Hydroponic farming is a method of growing plants without soil, using nutrient-rich water solutions. This advanced technology allows us to produce fresh, high-quality vegetables year-round while using 90% less water than traditional farming methods.
  • Where are Natural Harvest vegetables grown?
    All Natural Harvest vegetables are grown locally in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia using state-of-the-art hydroponic facilities operated by Nature's Harvest. We are proud to support Vision 2030's food security initiatives.
  • What types of vegetables do you offer?
    We offer a wide range of fresh vegetables including local varieties, premium hydroponic greens, and pre-cut chopped vegetables. Our product range includes lettuce, tomatoes, peppers, herbs, and many more traditional and specialty varieties.
  • How do I order Natural Harvest products?
    You can contact us directly through our website or call our sales team . We serve restaurants, hotels, food service companies, and retail partners throughout Saudi Arabia.
