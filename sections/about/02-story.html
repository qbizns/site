Our Story

Desert Agriculture
Sustainable Hydroponics

Desert Agriculture is a pioneering company dedicated to solving the desert kingdom's food security challenge through innovative, sustainable hydroponics systems. We specialize in advanced hydroponic farming that brings fresh, pesticide-free produce to Saudi communities year-round.

As a subsidiary of Aljoaib Holdings, we draw on nearly 25 years of experience in the agriculture industry to bring the latest technology designed specifically for desert climates.

Our Mission
To solve food security in Saudi Arabia by empowering farmers to grow anywhere with 95% less water through innovative hydroponics systems.

Our Vision
To be the leading provider of sustainable agricultural solutions in the Kingdom, setting new standards for water efficiency and crop quality aligned with Saudi Vision 2030.

Our Values
• Sustainability: 95% water savings, pesticide-free crops
• Innovation: Cutting-edge hydroponics technology
• Quality: Fresh, healthy produce at all times
• Community: Supporting Vision 2030 and local farmers
