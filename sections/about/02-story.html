Our Story

Natural Harvest by
Desert Agriculture

Natural Harvest is the flagship brand of Desert Agriculture, a pioneering company dedicated to transforming agriculture in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. We specialize in advanced hydroponic farming systems that bring fresh, locally-grown vegetables to Saudi communities year-round.

Our Mission
To provide the freshest, highest-quality vegetables while supporting Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 food security goals through sustainable agricultural innovation.

Our Vision
To be the leading provider of hydroponic vegetables in the Kingdom, setting new standards for freshness, quality, and sustainability in desert agriculture.

Our Values
• Quality: Never compromising on the freshness and safety of our produce
• Sustainability: Using 90% less water than traditional farming
• Innovation: Employing cutting-edge hydroponic technology
• Community: Supporting local food security and Vision 2030
